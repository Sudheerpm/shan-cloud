If you are an eligible AusNet Services customer you may request that the AusNet Services electricity meter at your home be enabled to permit a connection with a ZigBee® Certified and ZigBee® Smart Energy Profile (SEP) interoperable in-home device. In-home devices can be used to link appliances, or to assist you to better access and monitor your electricity consumption, which may help you better manage your energy usage.

You can submit a request to AusNet Services for your electricity meter be enabled to permit a connection with a ZigBee® Certified in-home device using this portal.

Before you begin your request, please ensure you have your electricity bill and device handy - You will be required to access information from these things during your request.

We are required to verify that you are the authorised electricity account holder, and we use many of the details shown on your bill to do this. You can help us by carefully entering your details exactly as they appear on your bill.
