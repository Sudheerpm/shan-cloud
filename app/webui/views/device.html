Your Device

Your chosen in home device must be a ZigBee® Certified and ZigBee® Smart Energy Profile (SEP) interoperable in-home device. You also need provide some information about the device you wish to connect to your AusNet Services electricity meter. This information is generally printed on the device or on the documentation provided with your device.

  • MAC Address (this may also be called a EUI-64 or EUID on your device)
  • Install Code (this Install Code could be between 20 and 36 alphanumeric characters)
  • Device Model
  • Device Manufacturer

You will also require access to your device during the connection process. Once your request is ready, we will send you an email to advise you.
